Bleu Cheese Burgers

These mouthwatering burgers are sure to be a hit. The tangy bleu cheese compliments the rich patty sensationally.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20)
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 green onions,chopped
  • 1/2 cup(2 ounces) crumbled blue cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Mix ground beef, cheese, and seasonings.
  2. Shape into patties and chill - 1/2 inch thick and wider than bun.
  3. Preheat grill or pan to high heat.
  4. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and grill 5 minutes per side.
