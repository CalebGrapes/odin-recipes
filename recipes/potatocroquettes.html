Potato Croquettes

Croquettes are fried mashed potatoes crisped to perfection which make a perfect appetizer or snack!

Let's dive in with these mouthwateringly scrumptous croquettes!

Ingredients

Mashed Potatoes

  • 1 pound (3 to 4) medium Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup finely grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped chives

Fry

  • 1 cup fine breadcrumbs
  • 2 large egg whites
  • Vegetable or canola oil, for frying
  • Salt, to taste

Directions

1.Make the mashed potatoes

  1. Peel and quarter potates
  2. Drain potatoes and add butter and pepper
  3. mash and mix until butter melts and you have a chunky mash(do not overmix)

2.Make and form croquettes

  1. Add parmesan cheese,egg yokes, and chives.Mix until combined
  2. Use a spoon to scoop out one golf ball-sized croquette at a time,then roll into balls

3.Fry time

  1. Preheat oil to 350 degrees, or medium high heat
  2. Add breadcrumbs to plate and place eggwhites in small bowl and whisk
  3. Dip croquettes one at a time into eggwhites, then into breadcrumbs
  4. Carefully put a few croquettes into hot oil until golden brown
  5. transfer to baking sheet or plate and sprinkle with salt
  6. serve immediately as is or with a dip!

Enjoy!
