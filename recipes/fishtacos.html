Fish Tacos

The Recipe

Spice up your next meal with the flavour of the seas!

These modern classic fish tacos are sure to please the pallette and transport you to the ocean with huge flavor. Follow along for the perfect fish tacos guaranteed to please.

Ingredients

Beer Batter:
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablepoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup beer
  • 1 egg
White Sauce:
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 1 lime,juiced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper,minced
  • 1 teaspoon minced capers
  • 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried dill weed
Fish Tacos:
  • 1 quart frying oil
  • 1 pound cod fillets, cut into 2 to 3 ounce portions
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour, or more as needed
  • Corn tortillas
  • 1/2 medium cabbage head, finely shredded

Directions

  1. Make beer batter: Combine flour, cornstarch, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl. Blend beer and egg in a separate bowl, then quickly stir into flour mixture until combined with a few lumps remaining.
  2. Make white sauce: Mix together yogurt and mayonnaise in a medium bowl. Gradually stir in fresh lime juice until consistency is slightly runny. Season with jalape√±o, capers, cayenne, oregano, cumin, and dill.
  3. Start fish tacos: Heat oil in a deep-fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  4. Dust fish pieces lightly with flour. Set aside.
  5. Dip floured fish pieces into beer batter. Set aside.
  6. Fry in hot oil until crisp and golden brown. Drain on paper towels. Lightly fry tortillas in hot oil until just crisped, but not too crisp. Drain on paper towels.
  7. Place fried fish in tortillas; top with shredded cabbage and white sauce.
